The History of Computers and the Internet

Early Computers

The first tools were abacus and other mechanical devices. Later, Charles Babbage designed the Analytical Engine, called the first computer idea. Ada Lovelace wrote programs for it. These early designs inspired the modern CPU.

Generations of Computers

The first generation used vacuum tubes. The second used transistors. The third had integrated circuits. The fourth brought GUI. Now we see AI and quantum computing. Example: E = mc2 and H2O.

Birth of the Internet

The Internet began with ARPANET. Later, Tim Berners-Lee created the WWW.
